Pyrus

Pyrus embodies the raw, untamed power of fire, originating from Vestroia’s blazing realm of infernos. This attribute is known for its aggressive, high-risk battle style, favoring overwhelming offense over defense. Pyrus Bakugan are often fiery in both appearance and personality. They thrive in chaos, their power surging in the heat of battle.

In the anime, Pyrus is the attribute of Dan Kuso’s Dragonoid, symbolizing leadership and courage.

Notable Bakugan

  • Dragonoid (Dan Kuso’s partner)
  • Helios (Spectra Phantom (Keith)’s Partner)

Battle Style

Heavy-hitting, relentless attacks with little regard for defense, often aiming to crush opponents in a single, fiery assault.

Correlation

The strongest opposing force of Pyrus is Darkus.
