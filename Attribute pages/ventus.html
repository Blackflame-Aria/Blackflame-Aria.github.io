Ventus

Ventus originates from Vestroia’s storm-swept realm, where endless winds carve through emerald landscapes. Ventus Bakugan are swift and agile, often designed as birds or ninjas (or both).

They control the battlefield’s flow, using speed and precision to outpace opponents. In the anime, Shun Kazami’s Skyress exemplifies Ventus’ cunning, often striking with surgical accuracy. Ventus Bakugan can summon gusts to disorient foes or gain tactical advantages, such as increased mobility or evasion.

Notable Bakugan

  • Skyress (Shun’s partner in season 1)
  • Ingram (Shun's partner in season 2)

Battle Style

Speed and control, focusing on manipulating the battle’s pace and striking with precision.

Correlation

The strongest opposing force of Ventus is Haos.
