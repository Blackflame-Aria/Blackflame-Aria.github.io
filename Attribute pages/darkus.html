Darkus

Darkus emerges from Vestroia’s shadowy realm of eternal night. Darkus Bakugan are mysterious and cunning, often designed with jagged, shadowy forms that exude menace. They excel in deception, using darkness to trick opponents and strike from the shadows.

In the anime, Alice Gehabich’s Hydranoid (initially used by Masquerade) embodies Darkus’ guile, often outsmarting foes with ruthless precision.

Notable Bakugan

  • Hydranoid (Alice/Masquerade’s partner)
  • Percival (Ace's partner in sason 2)
  • Hades (Shadow Prove's partner in sason 2)

Battle Style

Deceptive and manipulative, focusing on psychological tactics and direct trickery to undermine opponents.

Correlation

The strongest opposing force of Darkus is Pyrus.
