Subterra

Subterra comes from Vestroia’s rugged, earthen realm, a land of towering mountains and deep caverns. Subterra Bakugan are sturdy and grounded, their forms often resembling stone giants or burrowing creatures with heavy, rocky armor. They are known for their raw power and resilience.

In the anime, Julie Makimoto’s Gorem embodies Subterra’s tenacity, standing firm against overwhelming odds.

Notable Bakugan

  • Gorem (Julie’s partner in season 1)
  • Wilda (Mira’s partner in season 2)

Battle Style

High power with a focus on durability, using earth-based tactics to control the battlefield and overpower foes.

Correlation

The strongest opposing force of Subterra is Aquos.
