Aquos

Aquos hails from Vestroia’s ocean-covered realm. Aquos Bakugan are fluid and adaptable, they are masters of strategy, flowing with the battle’s rhythm to outmaneuver foes.

In the anime, Marucho Marukura’s Preyas embodies Aquos’ versatility, often shifting tactics or even attribues mid-battle. Aquos abilities often involve control, such as redirecting attacks or altering the battlefield with water-based effects.

Notable Bakugan

  • Preyas (Marucho’s partner in season 1)
  • Elfin (Marucho’s partner in season 2)

Battle Style

Adaptive and controlling, using the flow of battle to their advantage with strategic moves and environmental manipulation.

Correlation

The strongest opposing force of Aquos is Subterra.
